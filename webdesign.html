Web Design
Now what do you do? XEWAYS works with you to discover your current and future requirements of your web presence, and only then creates a framework of web design, content, and user experience.

Following tested usability principles we design sites that help clients encourage their customers to spend more time, explore services and demos, ask questions, and actually read content.

We help you focus your users' attention to specific areas of the site with a moderate use of visual elements, helping them to get from point A to point B without thinking of how it actually is supposed to be done.

Our web design is matched by our content development skills, where we work at both the conceptual level (demos, tutorials, and animation) and at the content level.

Using open source content management tools, we deliver rich business sites that clearly explain the client business and technology model, benefits, and ROI to the users - stories that they understand and remember.
