About Director

Dr. Satish Sharma - Founder Director

A techno-commercial professional with 24 years of cross-functional experience in the field of strategic collaboration, business development, new product development, quality management, process control & optimization, capacity improvement, inventory management, centralizing & up-gradation of systems and manufacturing operations in various engineering industries. After having worked with various Indian and multinational companies like Jabil Circuits/Celetronics, Multiarc/UCT, United Rubber and Sun Polymers at senior management level; Dr. Sharma started Xeways in 2011 that works as a strategic business partner to offer consultancy and services to SMEs and young entrepreneurs thru its global network of business partners. In his previous assignments, he has significantly contributed to enhance operational efficiency using standard management tools, centralizing systems, implementing quality systems and business diversification. He has been honoured with "Best Employee" Award for the year 2007 while working as Head - Test Engineering in Jabil Circuits/Celetronics.

He is Bachelor in Science, Graduate in Electronics Engineering, Post Graduate in Computer Science, Masters in Business Administration and Doctorate in Management Studies from the reputed Institutes of India. He is widely connected with various national and international associations. He is Member of Enterprise Management Association International Inc (EMA), Texas, USA,. he is Channel partner /Director in ( Food supplements, Commodities, Cosmetic,Ayurvedic companies in various part of india and international. for their new product development supply Chain management and direction in Business
